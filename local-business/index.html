Our story

A small kitchen with a big welcome.

Harbor & Hearth started as a weekend coffee cart and grew into a local dining room built around simple pleasures: good bread, careful coffee, generous plates, and staff who remember your name.

We work with nearby bakers, growers, and roasters to keep the menu fresh without making it fussy. Come in for a quiet morning table, a quick lunch, or an easy dinner with friends.

Locally minded Seasonal specials, fresh bakes, and a room designed for lingering.
